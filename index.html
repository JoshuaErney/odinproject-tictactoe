The Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Tic-tac-toe (American English), noughts and crosses (Commonwealth English), or Xs and Os (Canadian or Irish English)

A paper-and-pencil game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with an X or O. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row first is the winner.
